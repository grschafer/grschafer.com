What I got tired of walking across the building over and over one day, always finding the laundry machines taken. So, I decided to set up some Arduinos with a wifi shield and make a website to show laundry machine usage/status and, maybe, notifications (SMS?) for when your laundry finishes. The biggest unknown is how to detect current (or that the machine is running) without spending lots of money on current monitors or other expensive sensors.

Tech Processing with controlP5 for UI elements
What Inspired by Notch's hints at terrain generation in Minecraft, I wanted to see if I could replicate the landscape. Please note that it looks a lot better in OpenGL mode, which you can play in by downloading the source and running it in the Processing IDE.
How to play Play in the applet above, or download the source and open it in the Processing IDE
  • Move the mouse to "move" the landscape
  • Hold CTRL to move the mouse without moving the landscape (to get to UI elements)
  • Click and drag to move the camera

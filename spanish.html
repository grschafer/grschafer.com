Subjunctive Rocket Conjugation Game

How to play

Collect letters to correctly spell out the verb conjugated in subjunctive for the given form

  • Control player 1 (red) rocket with ARROW keys
  • Control player 2 (blue) rocket with WASD keys

What

So I'm cheating a little bit here, as this is a project related to school. The final project of my high school Spanish course was very open-ended, so I made a game where you drive a rocket to collect letters to correctly spell out subjunctively-conjugated verbs.

Tech

Flash + ActionScript 2.0
